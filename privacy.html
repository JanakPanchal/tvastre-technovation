3A. Collection and Use of Information

In order to improve the service quality and user experience, we will retain the information on your service use and related usage methods. In light of different functions of products and different fields collected due to systems, your information we will collect and save on your use of corresponding products/service is explained as follows:

(I) Personal information you actively provide in the process of providing you with products and/or services:

In order to realize the business functions or service at platforms as per shared list, we need to collect your personal information. If you refuse to provide the personal information required for realizing a certain function, you cannot use such function.

  • 1. Member/user login services: In order to register as a member and use our member services, the member/user name and password to be used, and email address must be provided to create a platform account.
  • 2. Advertisement push: We may, through methods including but not limited to system notifications, short messages, and emails, reasonably send you commercial advertisements. In case You do not wish to receive such information, You can unsubscribe.
  • 3. Customer services: When you contact us, we may record the conversation between you and us and collect other necessary information required for solving the problem.
  • 4. Cloud exhibition and Catalogue services: When you participate in an online cloud exhibition, we need to enable camera and microphone permissions. We will monitor video images in real time to ensure compliance with laws.
  • 5. Picture/video/file access and upload functions: You can use the tools for taking pictures and videos to upload materials to the platform.
  • 6. E-mail: You can chat with suppliers and buyers by email or platform messenger function.
  • 7. Instant communication function: You can chat with suppliers / buyers through user login on pages for product browsing, bargaining, and supplier display.

(II) Personal information we actively collect and use:

During your use of our services, we may automatically collect and store information in web logs:

  • 1. Equipment information: Including operating system version, MAC address, equipment environment, CPU, memory information and IP address, and location information (GPS location authorized by you).
  • 2. Service log information: Including browsing, click to view, trading, follow and sharing, information publishing, and operation logs.

Note: Separate equipment information or service log cannot be used to identify a specific natural person. If combined with personal information, it will be treated as personal information.

(III) Personal information we obtain from a third party:

We may collect and use your personal information from partners within the scope of your authorization. If you refuse such transmission, it may lead to disability to use corresponding services.

  • 1. Third-party login: We may share or obtain account information with third parties like Facebook, LinkedIn, Twitter, Instagram, or Google.
  • 2. Follow and share: We collect logs of your favorites, follow relationships, and share history to achieve social functions.

(IV) Equipment access permissions:

To realize business functions, we may request access to:

  • Access to microphone
  • Access to camera
  • Access to local pictures

Disabling these permissions in your settings will not affect other platform functions.

(V) Other purposes

We use your personal information to verify identity, prevent fraud, invite participation in surveys, and combine data for comprehensive analysis to provide personalized services.

(VI) Exceptions

According to relevant laws, we may collect and use necessary personal information without authorization if related to national security, public health, criminal investigation, or other legal obligations.
